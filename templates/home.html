Explore Featured Domains

Artificial Intelligence

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. In this scheme, you will work on projects that use AI to improve public services, automate processes, and inform policy. Mastering AI opens doors to high-demand roles in tech, research, and governance, making you a valuable asset in the future job market.

Cloud Computing

Cloud Computing delivers on-demand computing services over the internet. Interns will help manage and scale the digital infrastructure that powers national projects like DigiLocker. Expertise in cloud platforms like AWS and Azure is one of the most sought-after skills in the modern IT landscape.

Data Science

Data Science involves extracting insights and knowledge from large datasets. You will analyze national-level data to identify trends that can shape government policy. This skill is critical for any organization that wants to make data-driven decisions, placing you at the forefront of the information economy.

Machine Learning

A subset of AI, Machine Learning focuses on building algorithms that allow computers to learn from data. You could work on predictive models for agriculture, defense, or healthcare. This specialization is highly valued and leads to careers in building intelligent, predictive applications across all industries.

Video Editing

Video Editing is the art of storytelling through visual media. In this scheme, you will create compelling video content for major national campaigns like Digital India. Strong visual communication skills are essential in the modern job market for roles in marketing, media, and public relations.
